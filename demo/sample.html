Employee Leave Policy Specification

1. Overview

This document defines the accrual and carryover rules for annual leave. See Table 1 for the accrual rate schedule and Section 2.1 for proration rules.

2. Accrual Rules

2.1 Proration

Proration applies to employees who join mid-year. As per Section 3, the proration factor is computed monthly.

2.2 Ceiling

The maximum accrual ceiling is 42 days. Any balance above this ceiling is forfeited at year-end unless covered by an exception in Appendix A.

  • Grade A: 1.75 days per month
  • Grade B: 1.50 days per month
  • Grade C: 1.25 days per month

3. Carryover

Carryover is calculated at fiscal year-end. See Table 1 for the full accrual schedule referenced above.

Appendix A: Exceptions

Employees on approved sabbatical retain their full accrual ceiling regardless of the standard forfeiture rule in Section 2.2.
